Welcome to the Utilis

Utilis is an arts curation of the works of Brianna Vibar, a BFA Industrial Design graduate from the University of Santo Tomas. Part of her work includes designing products that not only meet the basic needs of its users, but also able to provide an overall enjoyable experience in terms of aesthetics, comfort, and functionality.
